About the App

A smarter way to share food in your neighbourhood

Every day, households prepare more food than they can consume. Meanwhile, community helpers such as security guards, housekeeping staff, drivers and domestic workers often go without a warm meal.

HoodEats bridges that gap. Donors post surplus food listings with pickup details and a time window. Receivers, anyone in the community, can browse and claim what they need. Simple, quick, and entirely local.

No money changes hands. No middlemen. Just neighbours helping neighbours.

"Would I serve this to my own family?"

The simple question every HoodEats donor asks before posting a listing. Because trust is the foundation of our community.

How It Works

From your kitchen to someone's table

Four simple steps, entirely within your community.

1

Sign Up

Create your account and set your role: donate food, receive food, or both.

2

Post or Browse

Donors list surplus food with photos, descriptions, and a pickup window. Receivers browse what's available nearby.

3

Claim & Confirm

A receiver claims a listing. The donor is notified. Pickup details are confirmed within the app.

4

Share & Connect

Food is collected at the agreed time. A meal is shared. Community grows stronger.

Key Features

Smart Food Listings

Add photos, ingredients, allergen info, and a pickup window. Listings expire automatically so nothing goes stale.

Flexible Roles

Switch between being a donor and a receiver anytime, from your profile settings.

Hyper-Local Matching

Food stays within your apartment complex or immediate neighbourhood. No long distances, no strangers.

Community Guidelines

Last Updated: June 2026

HoodEats exists to reduce food waste, strengthen human connections, and support the people who help our communities thrive every day. Our platform is built on trust, kindness, respect, and shared responsibility. By using HoodEats, you agree to follow these Community Guidelines.

Our Community Values

Reduce Food Waste

Every meal shared is a meal saved from going to waste. We encourage responsible sharing of surplus food that would otherwise be discarded.

Support One Another

HoodEats is about neighbours helping neighbours and communities supporting community helpers such as security personnel, housekeeping staff, drivers, domestic workers, and others.

Build Human Connections

We believe that sharing food creates stronger, more connected communities.

Act with Integrity

Be honest, respectful, and considerate in every interaction.

Food Sharing Guidelines

Share Only Food You Would Eat Yourself

Before posting food, ask yourself: "Would I confidently serve this to my own family?" If the answer is no, please do not share it.

Ensure Food is Fresh

Only share food that is freshly prepared, has been stored appropriately, shows no signs of spoilage, and is safe for consumption to the best of your knowledge.

Be Honest About Ingredients

Whenever possible, include main ingredients, vegetarian or non-vegetarian status, known allergens, and spice level. Transparency helps receivers make informed decisions.

Package Food Responsibly

Use clean containers and follow basic hygiene practices when preparing and packaging food.

Respect Others

Treat every member of the HoodEats community with dignity and respect. Use respectful language, be courteous in conversations, appreciate those who share food, and respect cultural and dietary differences.

If you offer food, be available for pickup as agreed. If you accept food, collect it on time and inform the donor if plans change.

Protect Privacy

Respect the personal information of other users. Do not share phone numbers without permission, publish addresses outside the platform, post photos or personal details of other users without consent, or use information obtained through HoodEats for marketing or solicitation.

Prohibited Content and Behavior

Unsafe Food

Do not share expired, spoiled, contaminated, or improperly stored food.

Misleading Information

Do not misrepresent ingredients, hide known allergens, or provide false descriptions.

Harassment and Abuse

Do not threaten, harass, or discriminate against users, or use offensive or abusive language.

Commercial Activity

HoodEats is intended for community food sharing only. Do not sell food without authorization, promote businesses without permission, or use the platform for advertising.

Illegal Activities

Do not share alcohol, illegal substances, dangerous items, or any content that violates applicable laws.

Reporting Concerns

Help us keep HoodEats safe. Please report unsafe food, suspicious activity, harassment or abuse, fraudulent behaviour, and violations of these guidelines. Reports are reviewed seriously and confidentially.

Consequences of Violations

To protect our community, HoodEats may remove content, issue warnings, temporarily suspend accounts, or permanently remove users from the platform. The severity of action will depend on the nature of the violation.

Together We Can Make a Difference

Every meal shared contributes to reducing food waste, lowering environmental impact, supporting community helpers, and strengthening neighbourhood connections. Thank you for being part of the HoodEats community and helping create a more sustainable, caring, and connected future.

Account Deletion Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

At HoodEats, we respect your right to control your personal information. This Account Deletion Policy explains how users can delete their HoodEats account and what happens to their data after deletion.

Requesting Account Deletion

Users may delete their HoodEats account at any time through the app settings or by contacting the HoodEats team. To request account deletion:

  • Open the HoodEats app.
  • Navigate to Profile.
  • Select Delete Account.
  • Confirm your deletion request.

Alternatively, users may email our team with their registered account details and a request for account deletion.

What Happens When Your Account Is Deleted

When an account deletion request is submitted:

  • Your profile will be removed from HoodEats.
  • Your login credentials will be deleted.
  • Your personal information, including name, email address, phone number, and profile details, will be removed from active systems.
  • Your food donation and request history may be anonymized for reporting, analytics, security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance purposes.

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained for a limited period where required by applicable laws and regulations, fraud prevention and security purposes, dispute resolution, or enforcement of our Terms and Conditions. Any retained information will be securely stored and protected.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 7 days of receiving the request.

Re-Registration

Once an account has been deleted, users may create a new account in the future using the same email address or phone number, subject to HoodEats' Terms and Conditions.

Email: help.hoodeats@gmail.com

We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing transparency regarding how your information is managed.
